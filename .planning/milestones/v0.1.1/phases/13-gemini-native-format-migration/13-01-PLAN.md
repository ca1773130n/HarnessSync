<objective>
Rewrite Gemini adapter's sync_skills, sync_agents, sync_commands, and _write_mcp_to_settings to write native format files instead of inlining content into GEMINI.md.

Purpose: Gemini CLI v0.32.0 natively discovers skills at `.gemini/skills/`, agents at `.gemini/agents/`, and commands at `.gemini/commands/`. Writing native files enables lazy-loading, tool integration, and proper slash commands instead of dumping everything into GEMINI.md.

Output: Updated `src/adapters/gemini.py` with four method changes covering GMN-07, GMN-08, GMN-09, GMN-11.
</objective>
